Medical volunteering program in Sri Lanka

Assists in Local Hospitals

Provides First Aid Training

Supports Medical Teams

Join our health camp to assist doctors and provide medical support to underserved communities. Provide essential medicines and medical supplies to those who cannot afford them, ensuring no one leaves without proper care. Good Health and Well-being

Good Health and Well-being (SDG 3) aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. It focuses on reducing mortality, improving access to healthcare, promoting mental health, and ensuring that everyone has access to essential healthcare services. Together, we can work toward a healthier, more equitable world for all.
