About SDG Goal 3: Good Health and Well-Being

The goal seeks to establish healthy lives and well-being for every person at every stage of life. The goal addresses significant worldwide health issues which include lowering maternal and child death rates, stopping disease outbreaks and providing healthcare services to everyone. The achievement of this goal depends heavily on providing people with essential healthcare services together with disease prevention and mental health support.

A world exists where all persons regardless of their background and location will achieve healthcare access through health promotion, innovation and collaboration.

Our Mission: Promoting Good Health and Well-Being

Good Health and Well-Being Initiative, exists to provide communities with essential knowledge, tools and resources that enable sustainable health development. Good Health and Well-Being Initiative works to make well-being a basic human right by providing education alongside awareness and action to generate positive life effects for people. Our shared mission involves developing a world where all people obtain better health and contentment.
