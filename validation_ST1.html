Good Health and Well-Being

SDG 3 Good Health and Well-being works to achieve health-related goals and well-being for all ages of people. The goal centers on lowering death rates while enhancing healthcare accessibility and mental health support and delivering basic healthcare to every person. The collective action of society will build a healthier environment where equity permeates every aspect of the world.
